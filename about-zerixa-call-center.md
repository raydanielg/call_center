## TOLEO LA KISWAHILI (Kamili)

### Kuhusu Zerixa Call Center

Zerixa Call Center ni mfumo wa kisasa wa Call Center unaotolewa kama huduma ya mtandaoni (SaaS - Software as a Service). Mfumo huu umejengwa mahususi kuwezesha kampuni, taasisi, na biashara za ukubwa wowote kuanzisha na kuendesha kituo cha huduma kwa wateja kikamilifu, bila kuhitaji kununua vifaa vya gharama kubwa wala kuajiri wataalamu wa IT.

Kampuni inajisajili, inachagua kifurushi kinachoendana na mahitaji yake, na ndani ya dakika chache inakuwa na call center yake kamili inayofanya kazi mtandaoni.

### Mfumo Unafanya Nini

**Usimamizi wa Simu (Call Management)**
Mfumo unarekodi na kufuatilia kila simu, zinazoingia (inbound) na zinazotoka (outbound). Kila simu inaonyesha nani aliyepiga, agent aliyeshughulikia, muda wa mazungumzo, na matokeo yake. Recordings za simu zinahifadhiwa kwa ajili ya ukaguzi wa ubora.

**Usimamizi wa Tickets**
Kila tatizo la mteja linafunguliwa kama ticket yenye namba maalum, kipaumbele, na muda wa kutatuliwa (SLA). Hakuna tatizo la mteja linalopotea au kusahaulika.

**Database ya Wateja (Contacts)**
Taarifa zote za wateja zinahifadhiwa sehemu moja. Agent anapopokea simu, anaona historia yote ya mteja huyo papo hapo, ikiwa ni pamoja na simu za nyuma, tickets zake, na maelezo muhimu.

**Kampeni za Mauzo (Campaigns)**
Kampuni inaweza kuandaa kampeni za kupiga simu kwa wateja wengi, kwa ajili ya mauzo, tafiti, au ukusanyaji wa madeni, na kufuatilia maendeleo ya kila kampeni moja kwa moja.

**Ufuatiliaji wa Moja kwa Moja (Live Monitoring)**
Wasimamizi wanaona hali ya kila agent wakati huo huo: nani yuko kwenye simu, nani yuko mapumziko, na nani yuko tayari kupokea simu.

**Udhibiti wa Ubora (Quality Assurance)**
Wataalamu wa ubora wanasikiliza recordings na kutoa alama kwa kila agent, kuhakikisha huduma kwa wateja inazidi kuimarika kila siku.

**Ripoti na Takwimu (Reports and Analytics)**
Dashboard zenye takwimu za moja kwa moja: idadi ya simu, muda wa kusubiri, utendaji wa agents, na mwenendo wa huduma. Ripoti zote zinapatikana kwa mchoro na zinaweza kupakuliwa kwa Excel au PDF.

### Nani Anatumia Mfumo Huu

### Kwa Nini Zerixa Call Center

- Hakuna gharama za vifaa, kila kitu kiko mtandaoni (cloud)
- Lipa kadri ya mahitaji, vifurushi vinavyolingana na ukubwa wa biashara yako
- Usalama wa data, kila kampuni inaona data yake tu ikiwa imelindwa kikamilifu
- Anza ndani ya dakika: jisajili, chagua plan, anza kazi
- Inakua na wewe, ongeza agents na huduma kadri biashara inavyokua

## ENGLISH VERSION (Full)

### About Zerixa Call Center

Zerixa Call Center is a modern cloud-based Call Center system delivered as Software as a Service (SaaS). It enables companies and organizations of any size to launch and run a fully functional customer service center without expensive hardware or dedicated IT staff.

A company simply signs up, selects a subscription plan that fits its needs, and within minutes has a complete, ready-to-use call center running online.

### What the System Does

**Call Management** - Logs and tracks every inbound and outbound call, including the caller, handling agent, duration, outcome, and call recordings for quality review.

**Ticket Management** - Every customer issue becomes a numbered ticket with priority levels and SLA deadlines, so no customer problem is ever lost or forgotten.

**Customer Database (Contacts)** - All customer information in one place. When a call comes in, the agent instantly sees the customer's full history.

**Outbound Campaigns** - Run sales, survey, or debt-collection calling campaigns and track their progress in real time.

**Live Monitoring** - Supervisors see the real-time status of every agent: on call, on break, or available.

**Quality Assurance** - QA analysts review call recordings and score agents to continuously improve service quality.

**Reports and Analytics** - Real-time dashboards showing call volumes, wait times, agent performance, and service trends, exportable to Excel and PDF.

### Who Uses It

The system serves five user roles: the Super Admin who manages the entire platform, Company Admins who run their own call centers, Supervisors who lead agent teams, Agents who handle calls and tickets, and QA Analysts who ensure service quality.

### Why Choose Zerixa Call Center

No hardware costs, pay-as-you-grow pricing, complete data isolation and security for every company, instant setup, and the ability to scale seamlessly as your business grows.
